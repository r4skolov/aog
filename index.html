About Angelina

Angelina Gorbaseva is the founder and CEO of AOG Home Builders Inc. Throughout her career, she has renovated and built several custom homes as well as built over 50 ADUs from ground up in Southern California.

She started AOG Home Builders when she realized there was a glaring gap in the construction and remodeling space. “A lot of my friends and family who were [embarking on home construction projects would complain about their experiences and I realized the relationship between a traditional contractor land homeowner was outdated”. As a result, Angelina established a company that would not only bridge this gap but one that would excel beyond the highest levels of professionalism and integrity in the industry. Under her leadership, the company has continued to grow and evolve into one of the largest design+build companies in Southern California.

Angelina was born and raised in Rostov-on-Don,Russia and moved to Los Angeles in 2013 to pursue her education in Business.

She is an avid tennis player. She believes that her true competitive nature and passion for innovation makes her one of the best at what she does. When Angelina isn't at work she's spending time with her family, traveling, playing tennis, hiking and considers herself a true foodie.
